To use the application, you need to allow the use of the microphone!

  • Open your browser and access the privacy settings. Depending on the browser you are using, the path may vary, but you can usually access privacy settings by clicking on the lock icon or address bar.
  • Look for the privacy settings related to the microphone and click on them. In some browsers, you may need to click on "Site Settings" or "Settings for this website" before accessing the microphone settings.
  • You should see a list of sites and applications that have requested microphone access. Look for the site or application that you denied permission and click on it.
  • Click on "Allow" to grant permission again.
  • Close the privacy settings and restart the browser.
  • Access the site or application again and allow microphone access when prompted.

Step-by-step guide to using Clear Sound

  • Access the Clear Sound website
  • Allow access to your microphone
  • Click the "Start Test" button
  • Say something into your microphone
  • Observe if the purple bars are moving in sync with your voice
